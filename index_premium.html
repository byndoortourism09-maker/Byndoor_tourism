🌊 Welcome to Byndoor Taluk
Byndoor Taluk is a stunning stretch of coastal Karnataka where the Western Ghats meet the Arabian Sea. From the world-famous Maravanthe Beach — where NH 66 runs between the sea and the Sauparnika river — to the divine Kollur Mookambika Temple, pristine Trasi beach, and thundering waterfalls hidden in the Ghats.
