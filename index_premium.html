🌊 Welcome to Byndoor Taluk
Byndoor Taluk is a stunning stretch of coastal Karnataka where the Western Ghats meet the Arabian Sea. From the world-famous Maravanthe Beach — where the NH 66 runs between the sea and the Sauparnika river — to the divine Kollur Mookambika Temple, the pristine Trasi beach, Navunda's tranquil river banks, and thundering waterfalls hidden in the Ghats — Byndoor Taluk is Karnataka's best-kept secret. The taluk offers an authentic coastal experience with warm hospitality, fresh seafood, and breathtaking sunsets year-round.
